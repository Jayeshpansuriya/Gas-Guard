Devices For Previewing & Presenting

The Gas-Guard circuit detects gas leaks using sensors connected to a microcontroller. When a leak is detected, it sends an alert to a central server via Wi-Fi or GSM. Users receive notifications through a web or mobile app in real-time, ensuring quick response to potential hazards.

Harts Of The Machine

The MQ2 gas sensor is at the forefront of safety technology, offering precise detection of a wide range of gases. Integrated seamlessly with Arduino Uno and ESP32, this sensor provides real-time monitoring and instant alerts, ensuring swift response to gas leaks or hazardous conditions. With its compact design and high sensitivity, the MQ2 gas sensor is the ideal choice for safeguarding environments with unparalleled accuracy and reliability.
